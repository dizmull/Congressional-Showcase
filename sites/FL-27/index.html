!! PetWatch !!

PetWatch is an app that helps pet owners keep track of their animals' health, vet appointments, medications, and daily routines. You can set reminders for feeding times, flea treatments, and vaccinations. There's also a community feature where local pet owners can connect to share pet-sitting, recommend vets, and organize adoption events.

I built it with Flutter so it works on both iPhone and Android. The backend uses Firebase for real-time data sync so your pet info is always up to date across all your devices. Because every pet deserves the best care!!
