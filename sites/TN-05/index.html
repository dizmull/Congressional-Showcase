About Me

I'm a junior at Hillsboro High School in Nashville, Tennessee. I've been a volunteer EMT with the local fire department since sophomore year, and it completely changed how I see personal safety. I care deeply about making sure teen girls feel safe walking alone, whether it's from school, a friend's house, or a late practice.

When I'm not on calls or studying, I'm usually stargazing from my backyard or binge-watching true crime docs (which honestly is part of why I built this app).

SafeWalk

SafeWalk is a personal safety app designed specifically for teenage girls. It lets you share your live walking route with trusted contacts, sends automatic check-in alerts, and has a one-tap emergency button that silently notifies your emergency circle with your GPS location.

The app also crowd-sources "safety ratings" for routes and streets based on user feedback, lighting conditions, and time of day. I built it using Flutter and Google Maps API, and it's currently being beta-tested by 40 students at my school.
