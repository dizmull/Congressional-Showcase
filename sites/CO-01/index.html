About Me

Hey, I'm a senior in Denver. I've been tutoring younger students since sophomore year and honestly the hardest part isn't the teaching -- it's getting everyone in the same room at the same time. I started coding because I figured there had to be a better way to organize study sessions than a group chat with 47 unread messages.

Also I drink way too much coffee and my favorite IDE is VS Code (fight me)

StudyBuddy

StudyBuddy is a web app that makes it easy to create, find, and join study groups at your school. You pick your subject, set a time and place, and other students can find and join your session. It also has built-in tools like a shared whiteboard, timer, and flashcard maker.

  • Find study groups by subject & time
  • Built-in shared whiteboard
  • Pomodoro timer for focus sessions
  • Flashcard creator & quizzer
  • Works on any device with a browser

Built with Node.js, Express, and Socket.io for real-time collaboration. Currently used by about 80 students at my school.
