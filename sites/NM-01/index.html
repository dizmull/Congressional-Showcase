About the Chef

Albuquerque, NM

I'm a senior at Eldorado High School and president of the cooking club (which I started freshman year with three people -- now we have 28!). I've been coding since 8th grade and cooking since I could reach the stove. My abuela taught me that the best meals come from whatever's already in the fridge.

I decided to combine my two passions and build something that could actually help families eat better without spending more money.

Today's Special: FridgeChef

FREE -- always!

FridgeChef is an app that lets you scan or type in whatever ingredients you have at home, and it generates real recipes you can actually make right now. No grocery run needed. It uses a recipe-matching algorithm I built that scores recipes by how many ingredients you already own.

It also tracks expiration dates and suggests recipes that use up stuff that's about to go bad. I built it with React and Node.js, and my cooking club has been the world's most enthusiastic beta testers. We've saved so much food from the trash!
