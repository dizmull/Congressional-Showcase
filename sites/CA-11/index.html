About Me

I'm a junior at Lowell High School in San Francisco. I'm really into transit systems and urban planning -- I've memorized most of the Muni routes and I spend way too much time reading about subway systems in other countries. I started coding in Python freshman year and haven't stopped since.

BusWhere

BusWhere is a real-time transit tracker built for actual riders, not just tourists. It uses GTFS data feeds to show you where your bus really is, predicts arrival times using historical delay patterns, and alerts you when it's time to leave so you never miss your ride again.

I built it because the official Muni app is terrible and I was tired of standing in the rain waiting for a bus that was 15 minutes late. BusWhere currently covers SF Muni and BART, with plans to add AC Transit.
