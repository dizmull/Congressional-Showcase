📋 MoodLog

track • reflect • understand

MoodLog is a daily mood tracking app that helps teens understand their emotional patterns over time. Each day you log how you feel using emoji scales, add optional journal notes, and tag what might have influenced your mood (sleep, social, school stress, weather, etc.).

Over time, the app builds beautiful visualizations of your mood trends and shows you correlations you might not notice yourself -- like that you're always anxious on Sundays or happiest after exercise. I built it with React and Chart.js, and the data stays 100% on your device for privacy. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
