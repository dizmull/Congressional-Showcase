About Me

Hi there! I'm a junior at Southwest High School in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I've been organizing school charity events since freshman year -- from food drives to park cleanups to holiday gift wraps for families in need. I believe that everyone has time to give, they just need to know where to show up!

I'm also on the student council, run a bake sale basically every month (my lemon bars are FAMOUS), and I genuinely think community service can be fun if you do it with friends.

VolunteerHub

VolunteerHub is a web app that connects students with local volunteer opportunities. You create a profile, set your interests and availability, and the app matches you with nearby events. It also tracks your hours and lets you build a volunteer portfolio for college applications!

I built it with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a Firebase backend. Right now it lists 50+ opportunities in the Minneapolis area, and three other schools have started using it. My favorite feature is the "bring a friend" button -- because volunteering alone is fine, but volunteering with your bestie is way better.
