💊 MedMinder

MedMinder is an app I built to help people (especially older adults like my grandma!) remember to take their medications on time. It sends friendly reminders, tracks medication schedules, and even alerts a family member if a dose is missed.

I built it with Flutter and SQLite after my grandma kept forgetting her blood pressure medication. Now she hasn't missed a dose in 3 months!! The app also logs medication history so you can share it with your doctor at checkups.
