About the Author

Hannah is a senior at Lincoln High School in Portland, Oregon. She reads over 50 books a year and runs the school library volunteer program, organizing reading drives and book fairs for underserved communities. Her bedroom has more bookshelves than wall space, and she wouldn't have it any other way. She believes every student deserves access to great stories, regardless of their family's budget.

BookSwap

BookSwap is a community-powered platform where students can trade books they've finished for ones they want to read. Instead of letting great books collect dust on a shelf, BookSwap connects readers in the same area and facilitates easy exchanges. Users list their available books, browse what others have, and arrange swaps at school or local meetup points.

The app includes reading lists, genre matching, and a reputation system so users can build trust. Hannah built it with Node.js and a simple database, and it's already being used by over 200 students across three Portland high schools.
