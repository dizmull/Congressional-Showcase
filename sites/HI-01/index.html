About Me

Senior at Punahou School in Honolulu, Hawaii. I learned to code entirely from free online courses — freeCodeCamp, The Odin Project, CS50, you name it. Growing up on the islands, I saw how many kids here don't have access to coding classes or tech mentorship. The nearest tech meetup is literally across an ocean. That's why I built CodeCoach — to bring the tech world to every kid on every island.

~ ~ ~ ~ ~

CodeCoach

CodeCoach is a peer-to-peer coding mentorship platform designed for students in Hawaii and other remote or rural areas. Experienced student coders sign up as coaches, and beginners can book free 30-minute video sessions to get help with everything from HTML basics to AP Computer Science homework.

The platform matches mentors and mentees based on skill level, language, and schedule. It includes a built-in code editor for pair programming, progress tracking, and badges to keep learners motivated. Built with Next.js, Socket.io for real-time collaboration, and PostgreSQL.
