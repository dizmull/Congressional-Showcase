About Me!!

Sophomore @ Mission Bay HS

Hiii! I'm Lily and I'm a sophomore in San Diego. I've been drawing since before I could write (my mom has proof). I run my school's art club and I'm really into digital illustration -- I use Procreate on my iPad basically 24/7. I love collaborative creativity, where multiple people add to the same piece of art and it becomes something none of them could've made alone.

That idea is exactly what inspired DoodleShare!

DoodleShare

draw together, anywhere!

DoodleShare is a collaborative drawing app where multiple people can doodle on the same canvas in real-time! You create a room, share a link, and everyone can draw together. It has brushes, colors, layers, stickers, and a chat sidebar so you can coordinate (or just be silly).

I built it with HTML Canvas, Socket.IO for the real-time stuff, and Express on the backend. My art club uses it for warm-up exercises and it's SO fun watching everyone's styles mix together. The best part? You can replay the whole drawing process as a timelapse!
