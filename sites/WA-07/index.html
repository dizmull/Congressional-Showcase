PlantPal

PlantPal is an app that helps you take care of your houseplants! You add your plants, and it creates a personalized care schedule based on species, pot size, and the lighting in your home. It sends you watering reminders, tells you when to fertilize, and even diagnoses common plant problems from photos using image recognition.

I trained the image model on over 2,000 photos of common houseplant diseases. Built with Python (Flask backend) and React Native. My test users' plant survival rate went from 60% to 94%!!

PlantPal currently has data on 200+ houseplant species
