About Me

I am a highschool junior in Egg Harbor Township, New Jersey. From a young age, I took an extreme liking to computer science and the brain, and have always wanted to make an impact on the world. Through BetterVeteran, I was able to assist underrepresented groups in my state and create a homage to my late veteran-grandfather.

When I was little, I always wanted to be remembered for doing something great. However, the older I got, I realized that giving that power of being remembered to those who feel forgotten matters so much more. I hope to continue advocating for the underrepresented voices in the world through my art, writing, and programming!

BetterVeteran

BetterVeteran is a no-barrier medical consultation assistant designed to support New Jersey veterans suffering from Traumatic Brain Injuries (TBIs), Intracranial hemorrhages, and PTSD. By providing no-signup-required AI detection and PTSD PCL-5 Mock Exams, BetterVeteran makes neurological assistance free for underrepresented groups.
