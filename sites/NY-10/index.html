About Me

Junior at Stuyvesant High School in Manhattan. I sometimes drive my dad's yellow cab on weekends, and let me tell you — finding parking in this city is absolute torture. I've spent way too many hours circling blocks in Midtown just to find a spot. That frustration is exactly what pushed me to build ParkSpot. When I'm not coding or driving, I'm eating dollar pizza and arguing about the Knicks.

ParkSpot

ParkSpot is a crowdsourced parking app designed for dense urban areas like NYC. Users report when they leave a parking spot, and drivers nearby get real-time alerts. The app uses geolocation and a clever prediction algorithm trained on street-sweeping schedules, event calendars, and historical data to show you where spots are most likely to open up. No more burning gas driving in circles — just park and go.
